Gender Bias In USA at 2020

By Group 2









Solutions to the Root Cause of the Problems at 2020 in the USA and Gender Bias

1. Emergencies commonly result in higher rates of gender-based violence. Prevent and address gender-based violence.

2. Women already spend 2-10 times more time on unpaid caregiving and domestic work than men. Address the disproportionate burden of women’s and girls’ care work.

3. Prioritise information and services on sexual and reproductive health.

4. Empower adolescent girls.

5. After more than 118,000 cases in 114 countries and 4,291 deaths , the Who declares COVID-19 a pandemic. To prevent you from being infected with a virus make sure you always wear a facemask to prevent you from being infected with the virus make sure you always wear a facemask.

6. Most people’s lives and work have been negatively affected by the crisis. The President supposed to help them by increasing the amount of their allowance.

7. Prioritise social protection and economic empowerment.

8. Invest in women’s and girls’ rights organizations.

9. Address the needs of women on the frontline of the pandemic.

10. Ensure equal leadership and participation by women, children and adolescents.
