Information about Gender Bias in 2020 at the USA



During 2020 in the USA, many faced criticism for their gender and also unfair treatment. In jobs, men were seen as better and more hardworking compared to women so that is why women has a decrease of pay whenever receiving their salary or pay. Studies have shown that for 2020 in the USA, for every dollar a man earns, that is around 80 for a woman. And getting a job overall will be harder for women since men are the spotlight and will definitely have higher chances of getting the job than women. Now just because women are seen as the weaker people that doesn’t mean that they can’t do anything because that is just the opinion of people. Although we don’t know for sure what women has invented or discovered during 2020, many women scientists has helped find the cure for Covid-19. And in general it would be impossible for us to live without women since they are the ones who give life or give birth to us and also raise us. So overall both women and men should equally be treated as for they both give advantages and an impact in our lives.
